About Genres Of Mangas

Anime and manga genres are groups of anime and manga works that are distinguished based on similar features of their internal structure.Many of the genres are similar to literary ones for manga and movie genres for anime, but they also distinguish specific works for this type.


Shonen: Manga targeted at tween and teen boys.

A dynamic plot, an abundance of humorous and combat scenes. Main topics: male friendship,
rivarly in life, sports, martial arts. The main characters are an example of decent behavior for
teenagers: they are purposeful, persistent, positive and come out victorious from any
unpleasant and dangerous situations.

Naruto

Senen is a manga by Masashi Kishimoto, telling about the life of a noisy and restless teenage ninja Naruto Uzumaki, who dreams of achieving universal recognition and becoming the Hokage — the head of his village and the strongest ninja..

Bleach

The action takes place in modern Japan. One day, in the bedroom of Ichigo Kurosaki, who has been able to see spirits and ghosts since childhood, a girl- the "guide of souls" ("shinigami") Rukia Kuchiki suddenly appears. After talking to Ichigo, Rukia was surprised that Ichigo could not only see her, but also touch her.

My Hero Academia

The plot tells about Izuku Midori, a boy born without superpowers (called quirks) in a world where they have become commonplace, but still dreaming of becoming a hero. He meets the Almighty, Japan's greatest hero and symbol of peace, who passes on his quirk to Izuku after recognizing his potential.
